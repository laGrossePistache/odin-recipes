Description

This is super quick ramen noodle coleslaw with sunflower seeds that is a favorite for potlucks.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) package broccoli coleslaw mix
  • ½ cup sunflower seeds
  • ½ cup slivered almonds
  • 1 (3 ounce) package Oriental-flavored ramen noodles, broken into small pieces
  • ½ cup canola oil
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup white wine vinegar
  • 4 green onions, chopped

Steps

  1. Combine broccoli coleslaw mix, sunflower seeds, almonds, and ramen noodles together in a bowl. Whisk canola oil, sugar, ramen noodle seasoning packet, and vinegar together in a separate bowl; pour over slaw mixture. Fold in green onions. Chill in refrigerator for 1 1/2 hours before serving.
